Abstract/Goal

The project's specific goal is to accomplish fundamental mobility functions including walking forward and backward, turning around in position, and changing one's body height. The robot will have three degrees of freedom on each of its modularly designed legs in order to achieve this. The robot's movements are flexible and adaptable thanks to its modular architecture.
